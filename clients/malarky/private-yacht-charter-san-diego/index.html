Private Yacht Charter in San Diego

Malarky charters are entirely private — the whole boat belongs to your group, not a section of it. No shared deck, no other parties, no tour guide with a megaphone. You and up to 11 others on 47 feet of catamaran, navigating San Diego Bay exactly the way you want.

Check Availability →Ask a Question

Private means the whole boat

When we say private charter, we mean it in the fullest sense. Your group books the entire 47-foot catamaran. No other groups are sharing the space. No strangers on the aft deck. The bow trampoline, the lounge, the cocktail table — all of it is yours for the charter window.

This matters more than people expect until they’ve been on a shared charter and realized the energy is completely different. Private charters have their own pace, their own music, their own conversations. You’re not managing around other groups’ preferences — you’re just living the day you planned.

Up to 12 guests, USCG certified

Malarky is Coast Guard certified for 12 guests maximum. That’s the legal limit and we don’t exceed it — not by one, not for any occasion. If your group is larger, our sister vessels can accommodate: Alana handles up to 35 guests, and Triton handles up to 100 on a 75-foot catamaran.

Route is yours

Your captain will suggest a route based on the day’s conditions and your group’s preferences. Romantic sunset sail toward Coronado? Active morning with paddleboards in the South Bay? Scenic circuit past the Midway and under the Coronado Bridge? All doable. The captain handles the sailing; you handle the destination brief.
