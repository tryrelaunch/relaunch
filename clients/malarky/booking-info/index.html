Your Charter is Confirmed

Here’s everything you need before your Malarky Charters experience.

Getting to the Boat

Our address is 2700 Shelter Island Drive, San Diego, CA 92106. The vessel is docked on the commercial dock just south of our office, behind Ketch Kitchen and Taps on Shelter Island. Parking is available in the adjacent lot.

Arrive 15 minutes before your charter departure time so we can get everyone aboard and underway on schedule.

What to Bring

  • Food — easy to handle on a moving boat. Charcuterie, sandwiches, takeout containers work well.
  • Drinks — BYOB in cans or plastic cups. No red wine. No glass.
  • Towels and sunscreen
  • A light layer for the breeze (especially for evening charters)
  • Soft-sole shoes or plan to go barefoot — no black-soled shoes

Not Allowed Aboard

  • Red wine (stains the deck permanently)
  • Glass cups or glass bottles
  • Black-soled shoes that mark fiberglass

Add-Ons

If you booked add-ons (paddleboards, lily pad, waterslide, kayaks) — confirm with your captain at the start of the charter. Best used when anchored in calm water; your captain will find the right spot.

Questions Before Your Charter

Call (844) 724-5787 or email info@malarkycharters.com. We’re typically reachable 8am–8pm daily.

Cancellation Reminder

Full refund if canceled 3 or more weeks before your charter date. Within 3 weeks, deposit is forfeited. Free rescheduling up to 1 week before departure.
