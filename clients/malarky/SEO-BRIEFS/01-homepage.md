## FAQ Block — Verbatim Copy

```
Q1: How many guests can charter Malarky?
A1: Malarky is U.S. Coast Guard certified for up to 12 guests plus our captain and crew. We're not a party boat — we're a premium private charter. If your group is larger than 12, our sister vessel Triton Charters can accommodate up to 100 guests.

Q2: Do you allow BYOB?
A2: Yes — Malarky is one of the only yacht charters in San Diego that allows you to bring your own beverages and food. Two rules: no glass containers (any liquid in glass gets transferred to plastic cups by our crew before boarding), and no red wine. Beyond that, bring what you want. Many guests bring a cooler of beer, a few bottles of champagne, and a charcuterie spread.

Q3: Is a captain included?
A3: Yes. Every Malarky charter includes a USCG-approved captain and crew. Unlike a bare-boat rental, you don't need a license or any boating experience — you and your guests just enjoy the day while we run the boat.

Q4: Where do you depart from?
A4: Our office and dock are at 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego, CA 92106. The vessel is on our commercial dock just south of our office, behind Ketch Kitchen and Taps in Shelter Island. Free parking available.

Q5: Where does the charter go?
A5: We operate exclusively in San Diego Bay. Your route is flexible — our captain will suggest options, but you can request specific spots. Most charters take in the Coronado Bridge, USS Midway, Star of India, Seaport Village, and the Embarcadero. We do not run open-ocean or Mission Bay charters.

Q6: How long should I book for?
A6: Two-hour minimum, but the typical Malarky experience is 3–4 hours. That gives you time to take in the bay, anchor at a swim spot, set up your add-ons (paddleboards, lily pad, waterslide), eat and drink, and head back. Anything shorter feels rushed.

Q7: Can you arrange catering?
A7: Yes — we work with local San Diego caterers and can arrange anything from light bites to a plated multi-course meal. Mention catering when you book and we'll send you menu and pricing options. If you'd rather BYOF, that works too.

Q8: What's your cancellation policy?
A8: For 2026 bookings: full deposit refund if you cancel 3+ weeks before your charter. You can reschedule at no charge up to 1 week before departure. Within 1 week, the reservation is non-refundable.
```
