## FAQ Block — Verbatim Copy

```
Q1: What does "private yacht charter" actually mean on Malarky?
A1: It means the entire 47-foot catamaran is yours and your guests' for the duration of the charter — no shared deck, no other groups onboard, no scheduled tour group around you. It's your music, your pace, your conversations. Captain and crew run the boat; you run the day.

Q2: How is this different from a ticketed harbor or sunset cruise?
A2: A ticketed cruise sells individual seats, so you're sharing the boat with strangers who bought their own tickets. Private charter is the opposite — you book the whole vessel for your group of up to 12. No strangers. We don't sell tickets to Malarky charters; the only way to book is private.

Q3: How many guests fit?
A3: Malarky is U.S. Coast Guard certified for up to 12 guests plus captain and crew. We don't exceed that — not by one, not for any occasion. If your group is larger, Triton Charters (our sister vessel) can host up to 100 guests on a 75-foot catamaran with bartender and dance floor. For mid-sized groups up to 35 with themed packages, see Alana Yacht Rental.

Q4: Where do you go?
A4: We operate exclusively within San Diego Bay. We do not run open-ocean charters and we do not operate in Mission Bay. Within the bay, your route is flexible — your captain will propose options based on the day's conditions and you choose. Most charters take in the Coronado Bridge, USS Midway, Star of India, Seaport Village, and the Embarcadero, with the option to anchor for swimming or water-toy time.

Q5: How long are charters?
A5: 2-hour minimum. Most groups book 3 to 4 hours, which is what we recommend — that gives time to get out, anchor at a swim spot, deploy paddleboards or the lily pad, eat and drink, watch the city pass by, and return. Anything shorter feels rushed.

Q6: Is alcohol allowed? What about food?
A6: Yes to both. Malarky is one of the only yacht charters in San Diego that allows BYOB and BYOF. Two rules: no glass containers (any liquid in glass gets transferred to plastic by our crew before boarding) and no red wine. Beyond that, bring what you want. If you'd rather have catering, we partner with local caterers — mention catering when you book and we'll send menu and pricing options.

Q7: How do I book and what's the cancellation policy?
A7: Book directly at the booking link or call (844) 724-5787. For 2026 reservations: full deposit refund if you cancel 3+ weeks before your charter, free reschedule up to 1 week before departure, non-refundable within 1 week of departure.
```
