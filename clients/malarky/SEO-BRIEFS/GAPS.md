## Photo library gaps

The Malarky photo library at `clients/malarky/images/` is missing dedicated shots of several water-toy add-ons. The new `/boat-rental-with-water-toys-san-diego/` page (per brief #06) ships with closest substitutes from the existing library, but conversion is likely to be meaningfully better once real photos exist.

Missing photos worth a dedicated shoot or social-media pull:

- **11-foot waterslide deployed** — the signature add-on, currently substituted with `water-deck-jump-03.webp` (group jumping from the deck). The slide is the most-photographed moment of most charters, per the brief; getting a real photo of the slide in use should be high priority. Bachelorette and family-charter conversion both hinge on this being credible.
- **15-foot lily pad in use** — currently substituted with general "people on deck" photos. The lily pad is the group-photo gravity center per the page copy; a real shot of guests on the lily pad behind the boat would carry significant weight.
- **Kayaks at anchor** — single and tandem sit-on-tops. No photo currently. Ideally guests in kayaks alongside the catamaran or paddling toward the bow.
- **Tower pads (diving platforms)** — no photo currently. Either platform from below with a guest mid-dive, or guests on the higher platform getting ready.
- **Family/kids using water toys** — for conversion credibility on the family-charter angle. The page currently uses `people-deck-fun-07.webp` which is good but generic.

**Suggested capture approaches:**

1. Next charter that's already booked with water toys → bring a photographer (or capable iPhone) for one hour of dedicated capture. Lily pad + slide + kayak shots in one anchor stop.
2. Pull from social media. Search Instagram/TikTok for `#malarkycharters` and DM guests for usage rights on their best slide/lily-pad shots.
3. Stage a half-charter specifically for content. ~2 hours at a Coronado anchorage with crew + a small group of friends/family + photographer. Captures all 5 toys in one session.
