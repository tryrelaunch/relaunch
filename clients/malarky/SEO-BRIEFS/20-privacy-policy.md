8. Your CCPA Rights (California Residents) — right to know, right to delete, right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell), right to non-discrimination
9. Your Rights (Non-California) — request access, correction, deletion
10. Children's Privacy — not directed at children under 13, COPPA compliance
11. International Users — services hosted in US, data may transfer
12. Changes to This Policy — how we notify, last-updated date
13. Contact for Privacy Questions — email, phone
