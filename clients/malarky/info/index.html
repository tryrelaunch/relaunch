Before You Board

Charter Info — Pricing, What to Bring, FAQ

Everything you need to know before your Malarky Charters experience: what to bring, where to dock, what’s included, and the ground rules that keep everyone safe and comfortable.

What to bring

  • Food — anything easy to eat and clean up. Charcuterie, sandwiches, snacks, a full spread if you’re feeling ambitious.
  • Drinks — BYOB fully welcome. Beer, wine (no red), spirits, mixers, non-alcoholic — whatever works for your group.
  • Towels and sunscreen — the bay reflects sun and wind comes off the water even on warm days.
  • Layers — San Diego can be warmer than you expect in the bay, or breezier. A light jacket is never a bad idea for evening charters.
  • Soft-sole shoes — avoid black-soled shoes that can mark the deck. Barefoot is also fine.

What’s included

  • USCG-licensed captain and crew for the full charter duration
  • Full use of the 47-foot catamaran including all decks and indoor lounge
  • Ice and a cooler for your drinks
  • Mini fridge in the indoor lounge
  • Bluetooth audio capability — bring a playlist

What’s not allowed

  • Red wine (stains the deck and cushions permanently)
  • Glass cups or glass bottles — plastic or canned only
  • Black-soled shoes that scuff fiberglass

Getting there

The charter departs from our commercial dock at 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego, CA 92106. Look for the dock just south of the office, behind Ketch Kitchen and Taps. Parking is available in the lot. We’ll send you exact dock instructions after you book.

Cancellation & weather

3 weeks’ notice required for a full deposit refund. Cancel within 3 weeks and you forfeit the deposit. Free rescheduling up to 1 week before departure. No refunds within 1 week of the event. Weather cancellations are handled case by case — San Diego has over 260 sunny days per year, so genuine weather cancellations are rare.

Add-ons

Paddleboards, lily pads, kayaks, tower pads, and a waterslide are available to add to any charter. Contact us when booking or email info@malarkycharters.com to confirm availability and pricing for your date.
