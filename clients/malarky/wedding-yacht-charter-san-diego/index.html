Wedding Charter

Wedding Yacht Charters in San Diego

An intimate reception, a rehearsal dinner, a post-ceremony celebration on the water — San Diego Bay is the kind of backdrop that makes a wedding feel like a film. Malarky holds 12 guests in genuine luxury: private, quiet, and utterly distinctive.

Intimate reception on the water

Wedding yacht charters work best for smaller, curated guest lists — the inner circle that deserves something beyond a hotel ballroom. With capacity for 12 guests, Malarky is sized for an intimate reception: close family, the wedding party, the people you actually want with you.

The boat creates a natural boundary — a shared experience that unfolds together rather than a venue where people scatter. The bay does the rest: the Coronado Bridge at golden hour, the city lights coming on as the sun drops, the sound of water against the hulls. It’s a setting that needs no decoration.

Rehearsal dinners and bridal parties

Malarky is equally well-suited for the day-before celebration: a rehearsal dinner on the water, or a bridal party champagne cruise. With no catering minimum, you can bring exactly the spread you want — a catered spread, restaurant takeout, a self-assembled charcuterie situation — and the crew ensures the setting is right.

Photography

The Coronado Bridge, the downtown skyline, Point Loma in the western frame — wedding photographers love the bay. If you’re bringing a professional, coordinate departure time around golden hour (roughly 90 minutes before sunset). The light is genuinely exceptional and the backdrop doesn’t require any setup.

What we provide

Captain and crew for the full duration, ice and cooler, full access to all three boat spaces. You bring food, drinks, decorations, and the people who matter. For wine, note that red wine is not permitted aboard — champagne, white, rosé, and spirits are all welcome in cans or plastic cups.
