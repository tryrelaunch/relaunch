Catamaran Charter in San Diego

A catamaran isn’t just a sailing boat with two hulls. It’s a fundamentally different experience — more stable, more spacious, and more comfortable than a monohull at the same length. Malarky is a 47-foot Voyage catamaran and the best sailing charter on San Diego Bay.

Why a catamaran

Twin hulls create significantly more beam (width) than a monohull of the same length, and that extra beam creates space — on deck, in the cockpit, in the salon, in the cabins. On a 47-foot monohull, twelve people would feel crowded. On Malarky, twelve people have room to genuinely spread out across three distinct spaces.

The other benefit: stability. Catamarans don’t heel (tip sideways) the way monohulls do in a breeze. Guests who get motion-sick on traditional sailboats often have no issues on a catamaran. The boat stays level enough that eating, drinking, and moving around are all comfortable even when the wind picks up.

The Voyage 47 specifically

The Voyage 47 is a blue-water catamaran from Cape Town — designed for extended offshore passages, which means the build quality, hardware, and safety systems exceed what most charter boats in this size range carry. On San Diego Bay, it’s overqualified, which is exactly the point. You get blue-water robustness on a sheltered bay, which translates to comfort, reliability, and a boat that looks and feels the way a luxury charter should.

What’s included

  • Full private use of the 47-foot catamaran — all decks, lounge, and spaces
  • USCG-licensed captain and crew for the duration
  • Ice and cooler (BYOB fully welcome)
  • Add-on water toys available: paddleboards, kayaks, waterslide, lily pad
