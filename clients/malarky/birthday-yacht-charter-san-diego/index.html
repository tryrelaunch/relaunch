Birthday Party on the Bay

Birthday Yacht Charters in San Diego

A birthday on Malarky is better than any restaurant private room, any rooftop bar, any hotel venue. San Diego Bay as the backdrop, a 47-foot boat as the party space, a captain so nobody has to drive — and BYOB so you bring exactly what you want.

Check Availability →Ask a Question

Why the bay beats any venue

Private dining rooms and rooftop bars offer a setting. A yacht offers an event. The moment you leave the dock, the charter becomes its own world — the city recedes, the bay opens up, and everyone on board is in it together. That’s the ingredient no venue can manufacture: shared movement, shared scenery, shared experience.

Malarky has space for 12 guests across three distinct areas. The bow trampoline doubles as a lounge and photo spot with the skyline behind. The aft deck has a shaded table for cake, toasting, and actual meals. The indoor lounge is climate-controlled and comfortable when you want shade or a quieter corner.

Bring the whole birthday setup

Decorations are welcome. Bring a banner, balloons (tied down so they don’t blow away), a cake (we have a fridge), streamers, whatever fits the occasion. BYOB fully applies — bring the birthday drinks of choice. No red wine or glass cups aboard, but everything else is fair game.

Milestone birthdays

A 30th, 40th, 50th, 60th on a luxury yacht in San Diego Bay is the kind of gesture that’s remembered for years. Several of our most enthusiastic reviews are from milestone birthday charters — it’s a different category of memory than dinner at a restaurant.
