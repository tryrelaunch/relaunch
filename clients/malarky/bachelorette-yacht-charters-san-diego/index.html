Bachelorette Yacht Charters in San Diego

The whole crew. The whole 47 feet. No strangers, no schedule, no shared deck — just your group and San Diego Bay stretched out ahead of you. Malarky is the bachelorette charter San Diego has been looking for.

Book the Boat → Ask a Question

San Diego Bay, entirely yours

Bachelorette parties on Malarky book the whole boat — not a section of it. That means your group has the run of the 47-foot catamaran: the bow trampoline for selfies with the skyline, the shaded aft deck for lunch and toasting, and the indoor lounge when you want a break from the sun.

You bring the decorations, the playlist, and the cooler. We bring the captain, the crew, and the boat. The crew knows how to pace a celebration on the water — when to anchor for paddleboard time, when to pick up speed and sail, when to give you a quiet moment in a scenic spot.

Up to 12 guests

Malarky is USCG certified for 12 guests — enough for the bride, the whole inner circle, and the spontaneous additions who heard it was a yacht and immediately wanted in. If your group runs larger than 12, our sister boat Triton Charters handles up to 100 guests on a 75-foot catamaran.

BYOB, food, and decorations welcome

Bring champagne, rosé, a charcuterie spread, a custom cake — whatever fits the occasion. No catering minimums, no alcohol package you’re forced to buy. The one rule: no red wine or glass cups aboard. Everything else is welcome. We’ll provide ice and a cooler.

Photography on the water

The Coronado Bridge, the downtown skyline, the open bay — there is genuinely no bad backdrop on this boat. Golden hour charters book fastest; if the date allows, an early-evening departure timed to hit sunset off Point Loma is a choice nobody regrets.
