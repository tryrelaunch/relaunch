Luxury, Done Differently

Luxury, done differently.

Luxury yacht charters come in all different styles. Some are built for large crowds, loud parties, and packed dance floors. Malarky was built for something more personal.

This is luxury at a smaller scale, your own private yacht, your own pace, and a day designed entirely around your group. By the second hour onboard, the crew knows everyone's name, your drinks are flowing, the music is yours, and the day starts to feel effortless.

It's relaxed luxury, the kind that feels natural instead of formal.

If your group is larger than twelve, our sister vessel Triton Charters hosts up to 100 guests on a 75-foot catamaran with bar service and a dance floor. For groups of 13–35 with themed packages, Alana Yacht Rental is built for that range. Malarky is built for the other end, a private yacht charter at twelve-guest scale.

The Vessel

Meet Malarky.

Malarky is a 47-foot Voyage sailing catamaran originally built in Cape Town, South Africa and designed for stability, comfort, and long days on the water.

The catamaran layout creates an incredibly smooth ride across San Diego Bay. The boat stays level and spacious, easy to walk around comfortably, relax with drinks, and enjoy the day without the rocking common on smaller yachts.

At 47 feet, there's plenty of room for your group to spread out and enjoy different parts of the boat throughout the charter.

Onboard features

  • Spacious indoor lounge
  • Covered aft deck seating
  • Large front bow nets over the water
  • Two bathrooms with full standing room
  • Four private staterooms
  • Bluetooth sound system
  • Mini fridge for drinks & catering
  • Easy water access for swim stops

The front bow nets are one of the most popular spots onboard, guests stretch out over the water while dolphins occasionally swim alongside the yacht through the wake.

Inside

Inside the yacht.

The interior of Malarky feels more like a modern waterfront lounge than a typical charter boat.

Large windows bring in natural light and frame views of the bay throughout the charter, while comfortable seating areas create a relaxed atmosphere for drinks, conversations, and meals onboard.

The indoor lounge becomes especially popular during sunset charters, when the skyline starts reflecting across the water and the entire bay glows during golden hour.

The private staterooms are available throughout the charter for guests who want space to change, relax, or step away for a quiet moment during the day.

The Bay

Cruise San Diego Bay.

San Diego Bay is one of the most scenic and calm boating destinations in California, the perfect setting for a private yacht charter.

Coronado Bridge

One of the most recognizable landmarks in San Diego, the Coronado Bridge creates an incredible backdrop for sunset photos, champagne toasts, and skyline views from the water.

USS Midway

Cruise alongside the historic USS Midway aircraft carrier docked in downtown San Diego, one of the most iconic waterfront views in the city.

Downtown Skyline

As the sun starts to set, the skyline reflects across the harbor and creates one of the most memorable moments of the entire charter.

Seaport Village & Embarcadero

The waterfront along downtown San Diego is filled with restaurants, marinas, sailboats, and classic Southern California harbor scenery.

Wildlife Sightings

Dolphins are commonly seen swimming alongside the yacht, while sea lions, pelicans, and other wildlife regularly appear throughout the bay.

Star of India

The world's oldest active sailing ship, docked at the Embarcadero, a quiet, beautiful landmark to cruise past.

Every route is flexible based on your group, the weather, and what kind of experience you want from the day.

On the Water

Anchor, swim, and relax.

Most longer charters include time to anchor in calm water so your group can swim, float, paddleboard, and enjoy the bay at your own pace.

Optional add-ons like paddleboards and the floating lily pad create the ultimate San Diego boat-day experience.

Guests often spend anchor time:

  • Floating with drinks in the water
  • Relaxing on the lily pad
  • Paddleboarding around the yacht
  • Playing music while anchored in the bay
  • Watching the sunset from the water
  • Taking photos with the skyline behind the boat

The atmosphere stays private, relaxed, and easygoing throughout the day.

Captain & Crew

Concierge-style captain & crew.

Every Malarky charter includes a professional USCG-certified captain and friendly onboard crew.

Your captain handles navigation, anchoring, safety, and route planning. The crew helps create a smooth, relaxed experience throughout the day, pouring champagne for a toast, helping plate catering, setting up water toys, taking group photos during sunset, whatever helps the day feel effortless.

Because the experience is designed for smaller groups, service naturally feels more personal and attentive than a large public cruise.

Captain fee is $50/hour, paid directly to the captain at the end of your charter.

BYOB

BYOB luxury.

One of the things guests love most about Malarky is the freedom to bring exactly what they want onboard. Bring your own:

  • Champagne
  • Wine
  • Cocktails
  • Craft beer
  • Catering
  • Charcuterie boards
  • Snacks & desserts

Many groups bring premium bottles they actually enjoy drinking instead of paying restaurant or bar markups onboard.

Whether you're planning an elegant sunset cruise or a fun birthday celebration, the experience feels much more personal when everything onboard is chosen by your group.

A couple of house rules: please, no drinking from glass on deck (pour into the plastic cups we provide), and no red wine (it permanently stains the deck). See the full BYOB charter details for more.
