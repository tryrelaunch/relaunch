Luxury Yacht Rental in San Diego

Malarky is 47 feet of blue-water catamaran — 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a modern indoor lounge, and every inch of San Diego Bay in front of you. The definition of luxury on the water is a boat that earns the word.

What makes Malarky genuinely luxurious

Luxury yacht rental is a phrase that gets applied loosely. Malarky earns it through specifics: a Voyage 47 catamaran built in Cape Town for blue-water voyaging, with the interior volume and build quality that implies. Modern decor, solid hardware, cushions that haven’t seen better days. A boat that looks the same in person as it does in photos.

The indoor lounge has comfortable seating, coffee tables, and natural light through the large windows — genuinely inviting, not a cramped below-decks cabin. The 4 bedrooms are actual staterooms, not bunks. The 2 bathrooms are clean and functional. The aft deck has a shaded table for eight. The bow has a trampoline net that hangs over the water.

Captain, crew, and a full bar situation

Your captain and crew are included in every charter. Crew can serve as a bartender on request — you bring the bottles; they pour and keep the pace. This is how private luxury charters work: your team handles the boat, you handle the occasion.

The right setting

San Diego Bay is the setting, and it delivers. The Coronado Bridge at sunset. The USS Midway from the water. The downtown skyline reflecting in the harbor. Point Loma dropping into the Pacific at the edge of the frame. You don’t have to do anything special — the scenery does the work. Your job is to be on the boat.
