Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Malarky Charters LLC ("Malarky Charters," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website malarkycharters.com and provides private yacht charter services on San Diego Bay. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what your rights are under California law (CCPA) and other applicable privacy regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information collected automatically as you use our website, and information from third-party service providers we use to operate our business.

Information you provide: Your name, email address, phone number, and any other contact information you provide when you submit our contact form, request a quote, or book a charter. For bookings, this also includes guest names (sometimes provided for charter manifests), charter date and details, and any special-request information you choose to share.

Payment information: Processed through our booking platform FareHarbor and its payment processor Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or full payment details on our servers; the payment data lives with the payment processor under their security standards.

Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type and version, device type, pages visited on our website, time spent on each page, referral source. This is standard website analytics data, collected via cookies and tracking pixels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing and managing your booking, including communicating with you about charter logistics, weather changes, and pre-charter coordination.
  • Processing payments through our booking platform.
  • Responding to your inquiries via phone, email, or the contact form.
  • Sending you a post-charter follow-up email (typically a thank-you and a review request).
  • Improving our website and services based on aggregated, anonymized analytics data.
  • Fraud prevention and account security.
  • Complying with legal obligations and responding to lawful requests from authorities.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your contact information with marketers or third-party advertisers. We do not send unsolicited promotional emails.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to understand how visitors use the site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use the following categories:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality (e.g., remembering your selections on the booking widget). Cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics tracks aggregated visitor behavior to help us understand which pages are useful and where users come from. Data is anonymized and aggregated; we cannot identify individual visitors from analytics data.
  • Third-party cookies: The FareHarbor booking widget, Instagram embeds, and any other third-party integrations may set their own cookies under their respective privacy policies.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only certain categories, or notify you when cookies are being sent. Refer to your browser's help documentation for specifics. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality (especially the booking widget).
