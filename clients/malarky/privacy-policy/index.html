Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

Malarky Charters collects information you provide directly to us when you make a reservation, contact us, or sign up for communications. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information processed through our booking platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process bookings and payments, communicate with you about your charter, send you relevant updates about your reservation, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Third-Party Services

We use FareHarbor for booking processing. Their privacy policy governs information you provide during the booking process. We may also use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Payment data is processed through our booking platform and is not stored on our servers.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at info@malarkycharters.com or call (844) 724-5787.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted to this page. Last updated January 2026.
