Party Yacht Rental in San Diego

A private 47-foot catamaran on San Diego Bay, entirely yours. No shared deck, no strangers, no time limits except your charter window. Bring whatever turns a boat into a party — we bring the captain, crew, and the scenery.

What makes Malarky the right party boat

Party boat rentals in San Diego range from cramped speed boats to massive commercial party barges with strangers on the same deck. Malarky sits in a different category: a 47-foot luxury sailing catamaran with enough space for 12 guests to actually spread out, move around, and have different experiences on the same boat at the same time.

The bow trampoline becomes a selfie station and lounging perch. The aft deck has a shaded table for eating and drinking. The indoor lounge is climate-controlled with a mini fridge. Three distinct spaces for twelve people — nobody’s crowded.

BYOB and food — fully welcome

Bring a full party spread. Coolers, cases, custom cocktails, a charcuterie board, a cake — whatever you want. We provide ice and a cooler on board, and there’s a mini fridge in the lounge. The only rule: no red wine or glass cups (bottles and cans only). Everything else is welcome.

Music and atmosphere

Connect your phone via Bluetooth and run your own playlist. There’s no mandated "tropical party" soundtrack playing on a loop — it’s your charter, your vibe. The bay provides the backdrop; you provide the sound.

Add-ons for the party

Turn the charter into a full water party: paddleboards, lily pads, kayaks, and an 11-foot waterslide are all available as add-ons. Best used when the captain anchors the boat in a calm spot — ask about the best anchoring locations when you book.

Also popular: bachelorette charters, birthday parties, and corporate outings.
