A Better Setting

A better setting for important conversations.

Some meetings deserve more than a conference room. Malarky was designed for smaller executive groups who want a setting that feels private, relaxed, and memorable without losing professionalism.

Whether you're hosting a leadership retreat, a board offsite, a client dinner, a partner celebration, or a milestone recognition event, the experience naturally feels more personal on the water.

With only your group onboard, conversations stay focused, the atmosphere stays relaxed, and the entire charter feels intentionally designed around the people attending.

The 12-Guest Advantage

Why smaller groups work better on the water.

Malarky is intentionally designed around intimate groups of up to 12 guests. That smaller scale changes the entire experience.

Instead of guests splitting into separate conversations across a crowded venue, everyone stays connected. Discussions feel more natural, relationship-building happens more easily, and the atmosphere stays genuinely personal.

For executive teams, client entertainment, and leadership groups, that intimacy becomes one of the biggest advantages of the charter. The setting creates enough separation from the office to help people relax, think clearly, and actually enjoy being together.

The Yacht

Meet Malarky.

Malarky is a spacious 47-foot Voyage sailing catamaran designed for comfort, privacy, and smooth cruising across San Diego Bay. The wide catamaran layout creates an incredibly stable ride, making the yacht feel more like a floating private lounge than a traditional boat.

Onboard features

  • Indoor lounge seating
  • Covered aft deck dining area
  • Large front bow nets
  • Bluetooth sound system
  • Four private staterooms
  • Two bathrooms with full standing room
  • Mini fridge & catering space
  • Multiple conversation areas throughout the yacht

The indoor lounge is especially popular during executive charters, a comfortable space for conversations, presentations, meals, or relaxing away from the sun while still taking in the panoramic bay views.

The Bay

Cruise San Diego Bay.

San Diego Bay creates one of the most impressive backdrops for executive events in Southern California. Popular sights during executive charters include:

Coronado Bridge

One of the most iconic landmarks in San Diego and a stunning backdrop during sunset and evening charters.

Downtown Skyline

As the city lights reflect across the harbor, the bay creates an atmosphere that feels upscale and relaxed at the same time.

USS Midway

The historic aircraft carrier docked along the Embarcadero gives the harbor a uniquely San Diego backdrop during the cruise.

Shelter Island & Harbor Island

Marina-lined waterfront areas known for luxury yachts, waterfront hotels, and incredible harbor views.

The route stays flexible throughout the charter based on your group, timing, weather, and the kind of experience you want from the event.

Captain & Crew

Concierge-style captain & crew.

Every executive charter includes a professional USCG-certified captain and experienced onboard crew. Your captain handles navigation, anchoring, timing, and route planning while the crew creates a smooth, effortless experience throughout the event.

The crew handles:

  • Catering setup & service
  • Drink service
  • Group photos
  • Event timing
  • Guest comfort
  • General hospitality throughout the charter

Because the charter is designed for smaller groups, service naturally feels more attentive and personal than a large public cruise. Professional discretion is part of the experience, NDA-ready crew service and private booking accommodations are available on request.

The USCG captain is $50/hour, paid directly to the captain at the end of your charter.

Catering & Corporate

Catering, drinks, & corporate coordination.

Executive charters aboard Malarky are fully customizable around your event style. Bring your own:

  • Wine & champagne
  • Cocktails & spirits
  • Catering
  • Charcuterie boards
  • Corporate gifts
  • Presentation materials

We also work with a roster of local San Diego caterers familiar with the yacht and the dock for groups who'd rather have full catering coordination handled. Mention catering when you book and we'll send menu and pricing options.

Corporate invoicing, purchase orders, and split billing are available for company bookings. Email info@malarkycharters.com with your PO details and we'll return an invoice within 24 business hours. Net 30 standard for established corporate accounts.

A couple of house rules for BYOB: please, no drinking from glass on deck (pour into the plastic cups we provide), and no red wine (it stains the deck). See the full BYOB charter details for more.
