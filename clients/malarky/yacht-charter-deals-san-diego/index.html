Yacht Charter Deals & Specials in San Diego

Malarky Charters offers competitive private yacht charter pricing throughout the year — not just seasonal promotions. Weekday rates, extended charter packages, and multi-hour deals are available to groups who want the full experience at the best possible rate.

Year-round value — not just Black Friday

Most charter “deals” online are limited-time promotions attached to specific dates. At Malarky, competitive pricing is standard — we believe a private sailing catamaran on San Diego Bay should be accessible enough that groups actually book it, not just dream about it.

Our weekday rates (Monday through Thursday) run lower than weekend pricing, making a Tuesday birthday sail or a Wednesday corporate outing genuinely budget-friendly for what you’re getting: a private 47-foot yacht, captain, crew, and the entire bay to yourselves.

Extended charter packages

Two hours is our minimum — enough time to get out past the Coronado Bridge and back. But the per-hour value improves as you add time. A 4-hour charter lets you anchor somewhere calm, deploy the paddleboards or waterslide, and still have time to cruise the full circuit of the bay. Groups that want to anchor and swim typically find 3 to 4 hours is the sweet spot.

What’s always included

  • USCG-licensed captain and crew
  • Full use of the 47-foot catamaran (all spaces, bow, aft deck, lounge)
  • Ice and cooler
  • BYOB and food welcome — bring whatever you want to eat and drink

Add-ons that make it a deal

Paddleboards, lily pads, kayaks, tower pads, and an 11-foot waterslide are available as add-ons priced separately. Adding water toys to a 3-hour charter is often the best-value combination — half the time sailing, half the time anchored and playing.
