More Than a Cruise

More than just a cruise.

Some yacht charters are all about the sightseeing, cruising the harbor, taking photos of the skyline, and watching the city pass by from the deck. Water-toy charters feel completely different.

Once the yacht anchors in calm water, the boat becomes your private floating lounge for the day. Guests jump off the swim ladders, relax on the floating lily pad, paddleboard around the yacht, kayak through the bay, and spend hours in the water with music playing in the background.

The cruise becomes part of the experience, not the entire experience. That's what makes water-toy charters some of the most memorable days on the bay.

Anchor Spots

Anchor in the best parts of San Diego Bay.

Water toys are deployed during anchor stops in some of the calmest and most scenic areas of San Diego Bay. Your captain picks the location based on the weather, water conditions, and the kind of experience your group wants from the day.

Coronado Cays

One of the most popular anchor spots for swimming and floating charters. Calm water, beautiful homes, and plenty of space to relax, paddleboard, and spend the afternoon in the water. Roughly 30 minutes from the dock.

Glorietta Bay

Located near the famous Hotel del Coronado, Glorietta Bay offers calm water with incredible views of Coronado, downtown San Diego, and the historic beachfront hotel in the background.

Behind Shelter Island

A closer anchor spot perfect for shorter charters or groups who want to maximize swim and toy time while staying close to the marina (~15 minutes out).

Each location creates a different vibe, but all are designed around relaxing, swimming, and enjoying the water at your own pace.

The Lily Pad

The floating lily pad.

The floating lily pad is easily one of the most popular add-ons on Malarky charters.

It's a giant floating platform tethered behind the yacht where guests lounge in the sun, jump into the water, relax with friends, and take some of the best photos of the day.

The lily pad naturally becomes the social center of the charter once the boat anchors. Guests use it for:

  • Floating and relaxing in the sun
  • Group photos with the yacht behind you
  • Jumping into the water
  • Hanging out between swim sessions
  • Relaxing with drinks nearby on the boat

For bachelorette parties, birthdays, and family charters, the lily pad often becomes everyone's favorite part of the day.

Paddleboards

Paddleboards on calm bay water.

San Diego Bay is one of the best places to paddleboard, especially for beginners. The water is calm, protected, and easy to navigate, making it fun even for guests who have never tried paddleboarding before.

Once anchored, guests paddle around the yacht, explore nearby shoreline areas, or simply float on the boards while taking in the views of the bay.

The crew helps with setup and basic instructions so everyone feels comfortable before heading out on the water.

One of the best parts of paddleboarding during a yacht charter is the perspective, seeing the yacht floating in the bay from the water creates some incredible photo opportunities.

Kayaks

Kayaks for exploring the bay.

For guests who want a little more adventure during the anchor stop, kayaks are a fun way to explore the surrounding water.

The sit-on-top kayaks are beginner-friendly, stable, and easy to use, even for first-timers. Guests often kayak:

  • Around the yacht
  • Into calm nearby coves
  • Along the shoreline
  • Around the anchor area for photos
  • Through calm water while the rest of the group relaxes onboard

Kayaks add a more active side to the day while still keeping the relaxed atmosphere of the charter.

Tower Pads

Tower pads and water jumps.

For guests who love jumping into the water, the tower pads add another level of fun to the day. Mounted higher on the yacht, the pads create a safe jumping platform into the bay during anchor stops.

Some guests spend the afternoon relaxing on the lily pad while others take turns jumping into the water, paddleboarding, or swimming around the yacht.

That balance is what makes the experience work so well, everyone naturally finds their own version of the perfect boat day.
