Charter Pricing

Yacht Charter Pricing in San Diego

Transparent pricing, no surprises. Malarky Charters rates cover the boat, captain, and crew. BYOB means your food and drink budget is yours — no mandatory catering packages or drink minimums.

Check Availability →Ask a Question

What you’re paying for

Every Malarky charter includes:

  • Full private use of the 47-foot catamaran (all spaces — bow, aft deck, indoor lounge)
  • USCG-licensed captain for the full duration
  • Crew (helps pour, plate, and serve throughout the charter)
  • Ice and cooler — bring your own food and drinks
  • 2 bathrooms on board

There is no mandatory catering package, no per-person drink minimum, and no gratuity policy that runs up the bill automatically. The charter rate covers the boat and crew. What you eat and drink is entirely your choice.

Charter duration and pricing tiers

Charter rates scale with duration. The 2-hour minimum gets you out on the bay and back. For groups that want to anchor and use water add-ons — paddleboards, waterslide, kayaks — 3 to 4 hours is the right window. Full-day charters are available for groups wanting an extended experience.

Check the booking calendar for current availability and exact pricing — rates vary by season and day of week. Weekday charters (Monday through Thursday) typically carry lower rates than weekend slots. Use the booking calendar or contact us for an exact quote on your date and duration.

Add-on pricing

Water toys are available as add-ons at an additional cost:

  • Paddleboards (set of 2)
  • Lily Pad (floating platform)
  • Tower Pad
  • Kayaks
  • Waterslide (11-foot)

Pricing on add-ons changes seasonally. Contact info@malarkycharters.com or call (844) 724-5787 to confirm add-on availability and cost for your date.

Deposit and cancellation

Booking requires a deposit. Full refund if canceled 3 or more weeks before your charter date. Within 3 weeks, the deposit is forfeited. Rescheduling is free up to 1 week before departure. No refunds within 1 week of the event.
