Team outings that actually bond people

Most team outings are forgettable by design. A charter on San Diego Bay isn’t. The environment removes people from their office context, the shared experience creates genuine conversation, and the format — moving through scenery, watching wildlife, feeling the wind — is one that most people don’t do in their daily life. The combination creates connection that a restaurant or escape room doesn’t.

Malarky holds 12 guests — right-sized for executive teams, sales teams, department outings, or a client/team combination. Small enough for real conversation; large enough for a meaningful group.

Client entertainment

Showing a client San Diego Bay from a private 47-foot catamaran is a different category of hospitality than a dinner reservation. The conversation is easier on the water — no other tables nearby, no noise, just the bay and the agenda you set. If the relationship is important, the setting should reflect it.

Logistics for corporate charters

The boat has Bluetooth audio for presentations or background music. The indoor lounge works well for small-group conversations away from the sun. Catered food and drinks are fully welcome — coordinate with a caterer to deliver to the dock before departure, or bring your own spread. We provide ice and a cooler.

For invoicing or corporate booking coordination, contact us at info@malarkycharters.com — we can accommodate purchase orders and advance billing.
