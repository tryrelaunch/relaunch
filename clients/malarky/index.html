About Malarky

San Diego’s finest luxury catamaran rental.

You and up to 11 guests will embark on an adventure through one of the most scenic urban bays in the country. Malarky is a Voyage 47 catamaran — built in Cape Town, South Africa — with a low-profile hull, ample sail power, and modern interior that makes for a genuinely comfortable day on the water.

Sit back and enjoy what San Diego Bay offers from the water: dolphins, gray whales, seals, the Coronado Bridge silhouette at sunset, and the USS Midway from a perspective most visitors never see. Your captain handles the sailing; you handle the good time.

The Spaces

Three places to watch the bay roll past.

01

Bow with Trampoline

Malarky’s unique bow offers plenty of seating and gorgeous views, keeping you close to the water. Watch the bay slip underneath while cruising. Dolphins tend to pace the bow — it’s the best seat on the boat.

02

Aft Deck

Shaded table seating for eight, great views off the stern, and easy access to the swim platform. Perfect for meals, toasting a special occasion, or just watching San Diego slide past at a civilized pace.

03

Indoor Lounge

A modern interior with comfortable seating, natural light, coffee tables, and a mini fridge. Retreat from the sun, keep drinks cold, or watch the skyline pass from inside — San Diego looks good through those windows too.

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know.

Malarky is Coast Guard certified for up to 12 guests. For groups of 13 or more, Triton Charters holds up to 100 guests on a 75-foot catamaran — same family of boats.
Every charter includes a USCG-licensed captain and crew, a cooler with ice, towels, and a bartender on request. You bring the food and drinks (BYOB is fully welcome — no red wine or glass cups). Add-ons like paddleboards and a waterslide are available separately.
You depart from America’s Cup Harbor at Shelter Island and cruise San Diego Bay. The typical route passes the Coronado Bridge, USS Midway, Star of India, and Seaport Village. Want a specific route? Just ask your captain — you’re not locked into a script.
Minimum is 2 hours. For the full experience — especially if you want to anchor somewhere and use add-ons — 3 to 4 hours is the sweet spot. Sunset charters typically run 2.5 to 3 hours.
3 weeks’ notice required for a full deposit refund. Cancel within 3 weeks and you forfeit the deposit. Rescheduling is free up to 1 week before departure. No refunds within 1 week of the event.
Our office is at 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego, CA 92106. The vessel is docked on the commercial dock just south of the office, behind Ketch Kitchen and Taps. We’ll send you exact dock instructions when you book.
