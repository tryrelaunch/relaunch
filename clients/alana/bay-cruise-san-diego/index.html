The Route

A complete loop of San Diego Bay's most iconic landmarks.

Every bay cruise follows the same route — and it's the right one. Here's what you'll pass in 2.5 hours.

🌉 Coronado Bridge

The 2.1-mile cable-stayed bridge connecting Coronado to the mainland. Alana passes beneath it — the arch overhead makes for one of the best photos of the trip.

⚓ USS Midway

America's longest-serving 20th-century aircraft carrier — now a museum at Navy Pier. Viewed from the water, the scale of the Midway is impossible to miss.

🏙️ Seaport Village & Downtown Skyline

The San Diego waterfront stretches along your starboard side. Seaport Village, the convention center, and the full downtown skyline reflect on the bay throughout mid-cruise.

🔭 Cabrillo National Monument

The western tip of Point Loma — where the protected bay meets the open Pacific. The lighthouse and monument stand above the cliffs. On clear days the view stretches to the horizon.

Departure point: 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego CA 92106 — behind Ketch Grill and Taps. Free parking on site.

Questions

Bay cruise FAQ.

Each bay cruise is 2.5 hours. That's enough time to complete a full loop of San Diego Bay — passing the Coronado Bridge, USS Midway, Seaport Village, and Cabrillo National Monument — without feeling rushed. You return to the Shelter Island dock at the end of your scheduled time.
Yes. Ticketed cruises are sold per person. You can buy one ticket, two tickets, or as many as your group needs — up to the boat's 35-person capacity. There is no minimum group size for ticketed cruises. Buy tickets online through FareHarbor or call (844) 320-1237.
The bay cruise does not include food service on board. The full bar is open and drinks are available throughout. If you'd like to eat before or after, there are several restaurants within walking distance of the Shelter Island dock — including Ketch Grill and Taps right at the marina.
San Diego Bay is a protected inland waterway, which means bay cruises operate in most weather conditions that would cancel ocean tours. In the event of a cancellation due to weather or operational reasons, tickets are fully refunded or rescheduled. The Alana team will contact you in advance if a sailing is affected.
Alana departs from 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego CA 92106 — behind Ketch Grill and Taps on Shelter Island. Free parking is available on site. Plan to arrive 10–15 minutes before your scheduled departure time so boarding runs smoothly.
