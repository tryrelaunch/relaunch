The Birthday Experience

Private charter. Your rules. Your occasion.

When you book a birthday private charter on Alana, you get the whole boat — not just a few seats. That means your guest list determines who's on board, you choose the departure time that works for your group, and the crew is focused entirely on making your birthday exceptional.

Bring the birthday cake. Set up decorations while the crew preps the bar. Board with your group, hit the bay, and spend 2.5 hours with stunning views of downtown San Diego, the Coronado Bridge, and the USS Midway — all with drinks in hand.

Catering is available for larger groups. Just ask when you book.
