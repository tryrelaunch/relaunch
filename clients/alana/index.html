The Vessel

40-foot Coral Island power catamaran.

Alana is a professionally maintained 40-foot Coral Island power catamaran — wide, stable, and purpose-built for groups who want a real party on the water. Two hulls mean minimal rocking, a wide open deck, and space for 35 guests to move around.

Every trip includes Captain Tess or Andrew at the helm, crew on deck, and bartender Ben behind the bar. The only thing you bring is your crew.

The Route

San Diego Bay Cruise Route

Every cruise departs from Shelter Island and circles some of San Diego's most iconic waterfront views. Here's what you'll see on the water.

🌉
Coronado Bridge

The iconic 2.1-mile span arching over the bay — best viewed from the water.

⚓
USS Midway

America's longest-serving 20th-century aircraft carrier, docked on the downtown waterfront.

⛵
Star of India

The world's oldest active sailing ship, moored at the Maritime Museum.

🏙️
Seaport Village

The bayfront shopping and dining district with front-row views from the water.

🗿
Cabrillo National Monument

The tip of Point Loma, marking where Juan Rodríguez Cabrillo landed in 1542.

🏖️
Shelter Island

Your departure point — a peaceful marina peninsula with scenic bay views heading out and coming in.
