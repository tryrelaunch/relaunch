Alana is a 40-foot Coral Island power catamaran based at Shelter Island in San Diego. She was built for one thing: giving groups of up to 35 a private party on the bay that feels nothing like a tourist cruise.

Every charter includes a professional captain, dedicated crew, and a licensed bartender behind a full bar. Craft cocktails, local beer, wine, and prosecco are available for purchase on board. No outside beverages, no ticketed seats, no strangers. You book the entire boat.

Alana runs seven days a week out of Shelter Island — birthdays, bachelorettes, corporate outings, family reunions, or just a good excuse to be on the water. The route takes you past the Coronado Bridge, USS Midway, Star of India, and the downtown skyline.
