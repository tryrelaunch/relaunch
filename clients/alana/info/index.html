Policies

Alcohol, cancellations, waivers, smoking, weather — what you need to know before you board.

No. Alana is a liquor-licensed vessel. All beverages must be purchased from our on-board bar. This is a legal requirement, not a preference.
Yes. On private charters, you can bring your own food, snacks, and cake. No outside beverages are permitted.
Full deposit refund if you cancel 3 or more weeks before your cruise. Rescheduling is available at no charge up to 1 week before departure. Within 1 week, deposits are non-refundable.
Safety comes first. If conditions are unsafe, we'll contact you to reschedule at no additional charge. Light overcast and cool weather are normal for San Diego Bay — we cruise in most conditions.
Yes. All guests must sign a digital waiver before boarding. You'll receive a link after booking with a QR code option available at the dock.
No. Alana is a non-smoking vessel. This includes cigarettes, cigars, and vape devices.
Yes. Children of all ages are welcome on private charters. Life jackets are available for children.

The Boat

Size, capacity, bathrooms, shade — what Alana has on board.

Alana is a 40-foot Coral Island power catamaran with two decks, two below-deck bathrooms, forward and aft seating areas, and retractable staircases for water access.
Up to 35 guests. Alana is U.S. Coast Guard certified for 35 passengers plus captain and crew.
Yes. Alana has two fully enclosed below-deck bathrooms.
Yes. The aft deck is covered with a hardtop providing full shade for most of the seating area. The forward deck is open for sun.

Practical Info

Parking, directions, accessibility, pets, and what to bring on the day.

Free street parking is available along Shelter Island Drive. There is also a public parking lot adjacent to the marina. Arrive 15 minutes early to find parking and walk to the dock.
2700 Shelter Island Drive, San Diego, CA 92106. Walk to the commercial dock on the backside of Ketch Grill and Taps. Look for the Alana sign.
The dock and boarding area can accommodate wheelchairs with advance notice. Please call us at (844) 320-1237 to discuss your accessibility needs so we can prepare.
Service animals are always welcome. For pets, please call ahead — we can accommodate dogs on private charters with advance notice.
Sunscreen, sunglasses, a light jacket or layers (it can be breezy on the water), your camera, and a valid ID if you plan to purchase drinks from the bar. Comfortable shoes recommended.
