Our Story

Built for the party. Designed for the bay.

Alana Charters LLC launched in 2020 with a single mission: give San Diego groups a private charter experience that actually felt like a party — not a guided tour. The result is Alana, a 40-foot Coral Island power catamaran that holds 35 guests, carries a full liquor-licensed bar, and comes staffed with a professional captain, crew, and bartender on every single trip.

We operate from Shelter Island — one of San Diego's most convenient departure points, right off the 8 freeway with ample parking and easy access to the bay. Our route covers the icons: the Coronado Bridge, the USS Midway, Seaport Village, and the Cabrillo National Monument shoreline. It's 2.5 hours of San Diego Bay at its best.

Since 2020, we've hosted birthdays, bachelorettes, corporate outings, anniversaries, graduations, and countless celebrations where the only requirement was showing up and having fun. The crew handles everything else.

What Makes Alana Different

Party-first design. Largest capacity on Shelter Island. Full liquor license (not BYOB). Captain, crew, and bartender included on every private charter. We built this vessel and this operation specifically for groups who want the entire experience, not just a boat ride.
