Group Planning

How to organize a group charter on Alana.

Large group logistics are easy when you know what the boat handles and what you're responsible for. Here's how it typically works.

1
Book the Boat

Reserve through FareHarbor or call (844) 320-1237. The boat seats 35 — you pay per charter, not per head. The more guests you bring, the better the per-person value.

2
Arrange Food & Catering

Outside food is welcome. Bring a catered spread, order trays from a local spot, or contact Alana to help coordinate catering before your sailing. Outside beverages are not permitted.

3
Decorations Welcome

Balloons, banners, centerpieces — decorations are welcome on private charters. Arrive 10–15 minutes early and the crew will help you get set up before guests board.
