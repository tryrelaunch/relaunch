Quick Answers

Common questions before you call.

How do I book?

Book online through FareHarbor — live availability, instant confirmation. Or call us at (844) 320-1237 and we'll take your reservation over the phone.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund with 3 weeks' notice. You can reschedule your trip up to 1 week prior to departure. Questions about a specific booking — email or call us directly.

Can I book a private charter?

Yes — private charters are our specialty. Up to 35 guests, the whole boat to yourselves, catering available. Learn more about private charters →

What are the cruise departure times?

Ticketed bay cruises depart Wed–Sun at 11am, 2:30pm, and 6pm. Each cruise is 2.5 hours. Private charters depart at custom times — contact us to arrange.
