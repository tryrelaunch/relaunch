Privacy Policy

How Alana Yacht Rental collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Last updated: May 2026.

This Privacy Policy describes how Alana Charters LLC ("Alana Yacht Rental," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website at alanayachtrental.com or book a cruise or charter through our booking system.

By using our website or making a booking, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when making a reservation or contacting us:

  • Name — to identify your reservation and communicate with you
  • Email address — to send booking confirmations, updates, and communication about your trip
  • Phone number — to contact you about your reservation if needed
  • Payment information — collected and processed securely by our booking platform (FareHarbor); Alana Yacht Rental does not store credit card numbers
  • Group size and event details — to prepare for your charter or cruise

We may also collect non-personal technical information automatically when you visit our website, including browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs. This information is used in aggregate to improve the site and is not linked to individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm your booking
  • Send booking confirmation emails and trip reminders
  • Contact you with updates, changes, or important information about your reservation
  • Respond to inquiries and customer service requests
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for targeted advertising.

3. Third-Party Booking Platform

Alana Yacht Rental uses FareHarbor as our booking and reservation management platform. When you complete a booking, you provide personal and payment information directly to FareHarbor. That information is governed by FareHarbor's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing their policy if you have questions about how your payment data is handled.

FareHarbor shares reservation details with us (name, contact information, booking details) so we can prepare for and deliver your cruise or charter.

4. Cookies

Our website uses standard browser cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them to:

  • Remember your preferences during a session
  • Understand how visitors use our website (analytics)

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features of the site may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking and contact information for as long as necessary to fulfill your reservation and comply with applicable legal and tax requirements. If you would like to request deletion of your personal information, please contact us at the address below.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. Payment processing is handled by FareHarbor using industry-standard security measures.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Links to Third-Party Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (including FareHarbor, Google Maps, Instagram, and Facebook). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies independently.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Your continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
