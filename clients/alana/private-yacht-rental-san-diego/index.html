The Experience

2.5 hours on one of the most scenic bays in the country.

San Diego Bay is bordered by the city skyline, Coronado Island, and Naval history. Your private charter takes you through all of it.

🌉 Coronado Bridge

One of the most photographed bridges on the West Coast. Alana passes directly beneath it — the scale is something photos don't fully capture until you're there.

⚓ USS Midway

The longest-serving Navy aircraft carrier of the 20th century. Viewing it from the water — rather than the dock — gives you the full sense of its mass.

🏙️ Seaport Village & Skyline

The downtown waterfront comes into full view mid-cruise. The skyline from the water is a backdrop you can't replicate at any land-based venue.

🔭 Cabrillo National Monument

The western tip of Point Loma, where the bay opens toward the Pacific. Clear days reveal the monument and the lighthouse standing above the cliffs.

Departures from 2700 Shelter Island Dr, San Diego CA 92106 — behind Ketch Grill and Taps. Free parking available.
