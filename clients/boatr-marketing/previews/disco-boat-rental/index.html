The Disco Boat is a vibrant 32' Suntracker Party Cruiser designed right here in San Diego to turn your day on San Diego Bay into a party to remember. With room for up to 12 of your favorite people, this boat brings the perfect blend of retro flair and modern comfort.

Step aboard and you'll find a custom 5-seat bar, colorful décor, and fold-down windows that let the ocean breeze roll in while the good times roll on. The boat's disco-style interior adds just the right amount of funky fun without going full time machine.

Whether it's a birthday bash, bachelorette party, or just a day you want to make special, the Disco Boat is here to make it extra fun, extra bright, and extra memorable.

Sunset Disco Ball Cruise · Golden Hour Signature

Sunset on San Diego Bay, with the disco ball lit.

The Sunset Disco Ball Cruise is the signature run on the Disco Boat. Depart Shelter Island before golden hour, head into the open bay, and as the sun drops the rooftop disco ball lights up and scatters sparkle across the water. Coronado in the background, downtown skyline behind that, the boat doing what it was built to do.

Cruise departs in the hour before sunset and runs through golden hour into the blue-hour minutes after. The Disco Boat is captained — your Disco Boat captain runs the helm at $50/hour, routing around the wind and finding the angle where the light is firing best. Your group stays on the rooftop watching the sky, not the chart.

Best for: milestone birthdays, anniversaries, proposals, brand shoots, end-of-conference send-offs. The mirror ball is the moment — bring the camera. The rooftop bean-bags + the bay backdrop is the shot.

Drinks: BYOB welcome. Cooler with ice provided. Music piped through the boat speakers (AirPlay / Bluetooth). Bring a layer for after the sun drops — the bay gets breezy fast.
