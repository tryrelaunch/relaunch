Four Ways to Get on the Water

Cruises and charters from Port Clyde.

Every cruise runs aboard the Laura B or the Elizabeth Ann, with the same crew that runs the year-round ferry. Reservations are required for all of them.

Call 207-372-8848 to set up a charter, or book the Puffin & Nature Cruise online below.
SEASONAL · MID-JUNE TO MID-AUG
Puffin & Nature Cruise

2½ hours out to Eastern Egg Rock, an Atlantic puffin nesting site and home of Audubon's Project Puffin. Seals, porpoises, minke whales, and a live lobster-trap haul with commentary.

$55 adult
$25 child
11:30 AM depart

Maine Lighthouse Cruise

An array of lighthouses including Marshall Point (you may know it from "Forrest Gump"), Whitehead, Two Bush Light, and Southern Island, now owned by artist Jamie Wyeth, whose studio is in the bell tower.

Sunset Cruise

Top off a summer day on the protected waters around Port Clyde. The water turns gold, sailboats and lobsterboats head for home, seals haul out on the rocks, and the sky can turn electric pink as the sun drops.
