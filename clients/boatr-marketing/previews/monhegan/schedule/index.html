Fares & What's Included

2026 fares, plainly.

No surprises at the dock. Here's what a crossing costs and what comes with your ticket.

Ferry fares

Adult round trip$52
Child round trip (ages 2–12)$35
Adult one way$35
Child one way$20
Pet$10

Two pieces of luggage up to 50 lbs each are included. Extra, oversized, or heavy pieces are $6 per item. Parking at the Port Clyde dock is $10 per day, with weekly and monthly rates available, just ask.

Reservations & refunds

Advance reservations are required. Tickets are held until a half hour before departure with full payment.

Payment is transferable to another date if you call at least 24 hours before your departure.

Payment is refundable (less a $5 per person deposit) if you call at least 24 hours before your departure.

Passengers only. No cars are allowed on the island.
