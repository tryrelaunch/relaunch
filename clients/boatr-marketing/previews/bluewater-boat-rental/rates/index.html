Policies · Read Before You Book

What's included, and the fine print.

Every rental includes use of the boat, safety equipment for your entire group including life jackets for young children, complimentary coolers and dive flags, a waterway map, a walkthrough of the local waterways on the large dock map, and an update on current ocean conditions. Ice and snorkel gear are available on premises.

Cancel at least 24 hours before your reserved time for a full deposit refund or reschedule. No call or email by then means store credit only, and cancellations after 5:00 pm EST the day before forfeit the deposit. Check the forecast the afternoon before to stay ahead of the cut-off.

We don't rent fishing supplies, but the Rod Room on Blue Heron Boulevard has gear and Seven Seas Bait and Tackle has live shrimp and frozen bait. Insurance doesn't allow towable watersports such as tubes, skis, or wakeboards with rental boats. Arrive 15 minutes early with your ID, credit card, and boating license if required.
