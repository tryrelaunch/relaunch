Do Bend right

You're walking distance from the good stuff

Most of our homes put you blocks from the river, the park, and downtown. Here's where to point your day.

Drake Park & the Deschutes

Grab a float or a paddleboard and drift the river, then sprawl on the lawn at Drake Park. Most homes leave you floats and a pump, so you just bring the sunscreen.

Downtown & the breweries

Bend runs on good food and better beer. Walk to coffee, dive bars, and a downtown full of restaurants. No car, no parking, no problem.

The Old Mill District

Riverside shops, the amphitheater, and easy paths along the water, about a mile from most homes, a flat bike ride on the cruisers we provide.

Mt. Bachelor & Cascade Lakes

Ski or ride 25 minutes up the road in winter; chase alpine lakes along the Cascade Lakes Scenic Byway all summer. Bend is a four-season basecamp.
