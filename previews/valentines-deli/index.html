About us

Named after
someone special.

Valentine's Deli is the vision of Kari Johannsen, a native Oregonian who named her deli after her mother, Jane Valentine — born on February 14th. Jane was all about the love of family, treating people with respect, and welcoming everyone with warmth. That's the feeling Kari wanted to recreate in her shop.

We're tucked into The Box Factory at 555 NW Arizona Ave in Bend. Open Monday through Friday from 11am to 2pm — just enough time to make you something genuinely worth your lunch break.

Every sandwich we make is crafted to order on fresh Sparrow Bakery bread, baked daily right here in Bend. No shortcuts. No pre-made anything. Just real ingredients from people who actually care.
