FAQ · The questions we get every week

Phoenix seeds & clones FAQ

Can you buy clones in Arizona?
Yes. Adults 21 and over can grow up to 6 plants per adult, with a 12-plant household maximum. We sell hand-bred clones for $30 each, in-person only, by Calendly appointment at our Tempe distributor. Mix and match strains at the appointment and save 10%. Follow the law — don't double the legal allowance. With autoflower seeds you can get 5–6 harvests a year, so be patient.
What's better — clones or seeds?
It depends. Seeds give you more flexibility — they're harder to introduce pests with, and autoflower seeds finish in 8 to 10 weeks (clones can't be autoflowered). If your clone provider operates in sterile conditions like we do, pests aren't a real concern. New grower? Start with autoflower seeds, always. Experienced grower with a clean tent? Our hand-bred clones are the fastest path to a heavy yield.
Do Arizona dispensaries sell seeds?
Some do. But it's not in a dispensary's interest to sell you genetics that yield like ours — every plant you grow at home is one you don't buy from them. We specialize in seeds and clones, so our interest runs the opposite direction: get you the best genetics on earth so you keep coming back season after season.
Do I have to buy a consultation to get seeds?
No. The old consultation-required model is retired. Order seeds online direct through our retail partner (The Seed Connect, ships from Tempe in 1–3 days), or book a clone appointment and pay $30 per clone at pickup. Bulk clone orders over 25 — email Colin directly.
Can I call to set up a clone appointment?
Yes — if you have a disability that prevents you from using our online booking, leave a message at 602-883-2672 and a team member will schedule you manually. Everyone else, please use the Calendly link.
What areas do you deliver to?
Seeds ship statewide across Arizona, typically 1–3 days from our Tempe distributor. Clones in Arizona are in-person pickup only — never shipped. New Mexico orders are handled by our sister site, marijuanaclonesonline.com (kept under the /clones-new-mexico/ portal here for continuity).
Can you set up my grow operation for me?
Grow tent kit setup service is currently suspended. Order one of our Mars Hydro or Spider Farmer kits, follow the included setup guide, and drop questions in the free Facebook grower group. We'll put a team member on your plants if you want — but only by special request. Email Colin.
