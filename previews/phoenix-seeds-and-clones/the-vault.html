Why The Vault drops every year.

Our breeder team has been chasing Apple Fritter phenotypes for years. Apple Fritter is one of the most stable, high-THC, easy-to-grow strains in the modern catalog — and our breeders have spent the last six seasons crossing it against the strains they actually want to smoke at the end of a long day.

Every year a handful of those crosses graduate from the test plots into the public catalog. The Vault is that release. We keep half the seeds for our own grow program; the other half goes out to home growers who want to run something that isn't available anywhere else.

What you actually get: 10 feminized seeds per pack. 90–95% germination rate on our process. Shipped from Tempe in 1–3 days. You handle the soil, the nutrients, the light schedule. We hand you the genetics that make all that work worth it.

Once any individual cross sells out, that's it for the year. Next year's drop is a different set of genetics. Order what you want now, or wait twelve months.
