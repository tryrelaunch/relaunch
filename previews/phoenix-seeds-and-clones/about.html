Phoenix's home-grower seed bank.
Built by growers, for growers.

When Arizona legalized adult-use home grow in November 2020, we brought our breeder team out of large-scale commercial cultivation and into a clone program built for the regular Phoenix home grower trying to grow their own medicine instead of paying dispensary markup forever.

How Phoenix Seeds & Clones got here.

Phoenix Seeds & Clones started in 2020, the year Arizona passed Prop 207 and adults 21 and over got the right to grow up to 6 cannabis plants at home. Before that, our breeder team had been running genetics for large-scale commercial cultivation operations — work that paid the bills but kept us anonymous and away from the actual home growers using what we were producing.

Legalization changed the math. Suddenly there were tens of thousands of Phoenix households allowed to grow their own — and almost no clone supplier doing it well. The dispensary clone market that did exist was thin, expensive, and the genetics felt picked-over. So we pivoted: same breeders, same standards, smaller plants, hand-delivered to the actual person who's going to grow them.

Today we run an appointment-only clone program at our Tempe distributor address to confirm, an annual feminized seed drop (The Vault), and a small grow-tent-kit retail line for first-time growers who don't want to spend their weekend assembling parts from Amazon. Seeds ship statewide in 1–3 days. Clones are picked up in person, $30 each.

Why in-person only for clones? Two reasons. First, AZ clone shipping is a legal gray zone we don't want any part of. Second — and bigger — clones are alive. You want to see them, smell them, ask the grower questions, and walk out with the plant you actually want, not the one a shipping label decided you got. The 2-hour Calendly window exists because every customer deserves real face time.

What we actually breed for.

Our team has been selecting Apple Fritter phenotypes for six years now. It's the most stable, easy-to-grow, high-THC starting point in the modern catalog, and almost every one of our Vault drops uses it as the mother line. The crosses you see in The Vault — Wedding Cake × Apple Fritter, GG#4 × Apple Fritter, GMO × Apple Fritter — are all phenotypes we've personally grown out, tested, and kept because they performed.

For the clone menu, the standard rotates weekly based on what's coming up healthy in the tents. We list the top eight on the homepage. The full list — Forbidden Boogie, The Soap, OG Kush, Georgia Pie, Louis 13, Sundae Driver, and 35 more — runs through the year as plants finish their cuts.

We don't sell what we wouldn't grow ourselves. If a strain comes up weak in our tents, it doesn't go on the menu. Simple rule.

How we run the business.

🌱

In-person, not shipped (in AZ)

Clones in Arizona are pickup-only at the Tempe distributor. Seeds ship — clones don't. Plants are alive; they don't belong in a UPS truck.

🤝

2-hour windows, real conversations

Every Calendly slot is 2 hours because we want time to talk strain selection, your grow setup, what's worked and what hasn't. No obligation to buy. No upselling.

💬

Free grower group, forever

Every customer gets an invite to our private Facebook grower group. Drop in with questions any time — your grow gets feedback from people who've grown thousands of plants. No fee, no upsell.

📦

10% off mix-and-match

Bring multiple strains home in one appointment, save 10%. Bulk orders over 25 clones — email Colin directly for wholesale pricing.
