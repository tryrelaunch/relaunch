What's in the kit · What's not.

A common rookie mistake: ordering a "complete" grow kit and finding out three days later that "complete" didn't include soil. Ours doesn't either — but we'll tell you exactly what you still need to grab.

Included in every kitWhat you get out of the box

  • Full-spectrum LED grow light (Mars Hydro TS-1000 or Spider Farmer SF-2000)
  • Reflective grow tent — 2′ × 4′ × 5′ or 6′
  • Inline duct fan, carbon filter, ducting
  • Mechanical timer for the light
  • Ratchet hangers for the light, the fan, the filter
  • Digital thermometer / hygrometer
  • Setup guide and our Facebook grower group invite

Not in the kitWhat you still need to buy

  • Seeds or clones — pick from our Vault drops, autoflowers from The Seed Connect, or book a clone appointment
  • Soil — 5 to 7 gallons of quality grow soil per plant (FoxFarm Ocean Forest is the easiest starting point)
  • Pots — 5-gallon fabric pots, 4 of them
  • Nutrients — General Hydroponics Flora trio is the easiest beginner combo
  • pH meter and pH adjusters (up / down)
  • Watering can or simple sprayer

Need help picking a kit?

Both kits will give a healthy plant. Mars Hydro is the workhorse — heavy yield, easy LED, free Apple Fritter seeds. Spider Farmer's light is technically nicer (Samsung diodes, dimmable) for grow nerds who want to push the cycle. If this is your first grow, get the Mars Hydro and email Colin if you want him to walk you through setup.
