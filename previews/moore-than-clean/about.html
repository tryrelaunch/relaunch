About Us

Meet Jacob Moore

Our Mission

Family-Owned, Eco-Friendly

Moore Than Clean is a family-owned business committed to providing exceptional carpet cleaning in Prineville, Oregon, as well as top-notch upholstery cleaning services. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, eco-friendly carpet cleaning solutions that are family and pet friendly.

We use state of the art products that are tough on dirt, but gentle on your family and belongings. We strive to not only meet, but exceed our customers' expectations.

That mission isn't a slogan — it's the reason this business exists. When you let someone into your home with cleaning equipment and chemicals, you should know what they're using and why. Our products are eco-friendly because that's the standard we hold for our own family. The work we do for your home is the same work we'd do for ours.
