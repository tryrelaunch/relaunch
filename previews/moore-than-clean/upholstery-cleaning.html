Upholstery Cleaning in Prineville, Oregon

In addition to carpet cleaning, Moore Than Clean also offers upholstery cleaning services. We can clean all types of furniture, including sofas, armchairs, and dining chairs. Our cleaning process removes dirt, stains, and allergens, leaving your furniture looking and feeling like new.

Furniture That Looks (and Feels) Like New

Upholstery doesn't just hold dirt — it traps body oils, food residue, dust mites, pet dander, and the slow accumulation of life. Vacuuming gets the surface. Hot water extraction gets everything else. We use the same eco-friendly, family- and pet-safe products on your furniture that we use on your carpet.

What We Clean

  • Sofas & loveseats (microfiber, cotton, linen, polyester)
  • Sectionals & modular pieces
  • Armchairs, recliners, & accent chairs
  • Dining chairs & bar stools (whole sets)
  • Ottomans & benches
  • Office chairs & cubicle seating
  • Restaurant booth seating & banquettes
  • Auto interiors & RV upholstery (see also Auto & RV)

Our Process

1. Fabric inspection. We identify the fabric type and any cleaning code on the manufacturer label. This determines the right method — hot water extraction for most synthetics and cottons, dry methods for delicate or specialty fabrics.

2. Spot & stain pre-treatment. Targeted treatment for problem areas before the main cleaning pass.

3. Hot water extraction. Truck-mounted equipment with controlled pressure and temperature so we don't oversaturate.

4. Grooming & finish. Where appropriate, we groom fibers so the piece dries evenly.

5. Optional protection. Add eco-friendly fabric protectant to keep new spills from setting in.

Allergens, Dust Mites, & Air Quality

If anyone in your household struggles with allergies, your couch is probably part of the problem. A typical sofa accumulates pollen, dust mites, dander, and mold spores over time. Professional extraction pulls these out of the fibers — most clients notice the difference in air quality the same week.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to dry? 2 to 4 hours to the touch, 6 to 8 hours fully. Faster with fans.

What fabrics can you clean? Most fabrics — cotton, polyester, microfiber, linen, wool blends. Leather and silk we approach with specialty methods.

Do you remove pet stains and odors? Yes — same urine and odor treatment as carpet, just applied to upholstery.

Can you clean a whole dining set? Absolutely. Chairs, table seating, and bar stools all in one visit.
