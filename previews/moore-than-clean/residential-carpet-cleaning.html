Residential Carpet Cleaning in Prineville, Oregon

Buying a new home? Selling one? Preparing for the holidays? If you'd like to erase the past that's trapped in your carpets, Moore Than Clean offers professional carpet cleaning services to reverse the clock.

The Moore Than Clean Difference

For most carpets, professional cleaning is the difference between a home that looks okay and one that feels clean. Vacuuming pulls surface debris. Steam extraction pulls what vacuums can't reach — embedded dirt, oils, allergens, and the slow buildup of pet, kid, and life messes that dull the fibers over time.

Moore Than Clean is family-owned, locally operated, and based right here in Prineville. We use eco-friendly products that are tough on dirt but gentle on your family and belongings — same standard we use in our own house.

What's Included in a Residential Cleaning

  • Pre-inspection & problem-area identification
  • Furniture moved (standard items, by request)
  • Pre-treatment for spots, stains, and high-traffic areas
  • Truck-mounted hot water extraction (deep clean)
  • Eco-friendly, family- and pet-safe products
  • Post-cleaning grooming for an even, fresh finish
  • Optional: fabric protection & deodorizing

Our Process, Step by Step

1. Free estimate. Call or text us with your square footage and rooms. We'll give you a quote on the spot or schedule a quick walkthrough.

2. Schedule. We service Prineville, Bend, Redmond, Madras, and surrounding areas. Same-day appointments are often available.

3. Walk the home. We'll check problem areas with you — pet stains, traffic patterns, anything you've been worried about — and confirm the plan.

4. Clean. Pre-treatment first, then truck-mounted hot water extraction. We work room by room, methodical and thorough.

5. Walkthrough. Before we leave, we walk the home with you to confirm you're satisfied. No surprises, no missed spots.

Carpet Cleaning for Every Situation

Moving in or out: Reset the carpets to like-new before you live with someone else's history — or before you hand the keys over.

Holidays & entertaining: Get the house guest-ready without a frantic last-minute scrub.

Pet households: Even well-trained pets leave a mark over time. Our urine and odor treatments handle what most products can't.

Allergies & air quality: Deep extraction pulls dust mites, pollen, and dander out of the fibers — your HVAC will thank you.

Annual refresh: Most manufacturers recommend professional cleaning every 12 months to keep warranties valid and fibers looking right.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take? Most homes take 1 to 3 hours depending on size and soiling.

How long until I can walk on it? A few hours. Full drying is 6 to 12 hours depending on humidity and airflow.

Are your products safe for kids and pets? Yes — that's the whole point. Family- and pet-safe is non-negotiable for us.

Do you move furniture? We move standard items. Beds, dressers, and pianos we usually clean around.

What areas do you serve? Prineville, Bend, Redmond, Madras, and surrounding Central Oregon. Call if you're unsure — we'll let you know.
