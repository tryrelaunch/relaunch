Built Around Your Business Hours

The hardest part of commercial carpet cleaning isn't the cleaning — it's the scheduling. Most businesses can't shut down operations to let a crew take over, and most crews can't (or won't) work the hours that don't disrupt your day. Moore Than Clean does both.

We schedule around your business. Early mornings before you open. Evenings after the last employee leaves. Weekends when the lights are off. Recurring contracts get the slots they need to keep things running smoothly.

Recurring Service Plans

Most commercial spaces look their best on a recurring schedule. We build plans around how heavily your space is used:

Quarterly: A solid baseline for most office, retail, and small commercial spaces.

Bi-monthly: The right cadence for restaurants, gyms, and high-touch retail.

Monthly: For very high-traffic environments or businesses where appearance is part of the brand.

Recurring clients get preferred scheduling, locked-in pricing, and a heads-up on upcoming visits — no surprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer after-hours service? Yes. Evenings, mornings, weekends — we schedule around you.

Do you have recurring contracts? Yes — preferred scheduling and locked pricing for recurring clients.

Pricing? Commercial pricing is per square foot and depends on traffic and frequency. Call for a fast quote.
