Press & Accolades

The reviews are in.

Celebrity-chef ownership, a nationally recognized executive chef, a craft bar program, and one of Central Oregon's most active private-events departments — here's what people are saying about Hawkeye & Huckleberry Lounge.

Recognition

Restaurant recognition & notable mentions

  • Featured by Bend Magazine as one of Bend's most anticipated restaurant openings and a standout addition to the city's dining scene.
  • Featured in The Source Weekly's Happy Hour Guide, highlighting Hawk & Huck's daily happy hour and cocktail program.
  • Highlighted by Condé Nast Traveler as one of Bend's notable dining destinations.
  • Frequently recognized for its “modern cowboy steakhouse” concept, live-music programming, ranch-to-plate menu, and event-driven atmosphere.
  • Named Rookie Restaurant of the Year by The Source Weekly.
  • Featured by Eater Portland for bringing celebrity-chef-driven dining back to Central Oregon.
Bar Program

Happy hour, cocktails & mocktails

  • Featured as a destination happy hour in Bend by The Source Weekly. The daily Happy Hour (4–5 PM) has been specifically highlighted across multiple publications covering Bend dining and nightlife.
  • The Cool Hand Luke mocktail has been publicly highlighted as a standout zero-proof offering, referenced alongside “Best Mocktail” recognition on social media.
  • Signature cocktails such as the Tumalo Honey and I'm Your Huckleberry have been featured in local media and restaurant coverage.
Executive Chef

Carlos Anthony — Food Network personality & Michelin-recommended chef

  • Named to the Food Network Hot List 2025 as a nationally recognized Food Network personality.
  • Michelin-recommended chef, with six consecutive years of Michelin recognition during his tenure at Herb & Wood in San Diego.
  • Winner of Chopped: Next Generation (2022).
  • Winner of Cutthroat Kitchen.
  • Competitor on Tournament of Champions.
  • Competitor on 24 in 24: Last Chef Standing.
  • Appeared on Guy's Grocery Games.
  • Competed on Superchef Grudge Match against mentor Brian Malarkey.
  • Recognized nationally through Food Network competition success, and featured by Central Oregon media as one of the region's most visible culinary talents.
Owner

Brian Malarkey — television & national recognition

  • Finalist on Top Chef.
  • Food Network celebrity chef who has opened over 20 restaurants across the country.
  • Judge and recurring personality across numerous Food Network programs.
  • Competitor and mentor figure on multiple national cooking competitions.
  • A nationally recognized restaurateur with decades of industry leadership across the country.
  • Returned to his home state of Oregon to create Hawkeye & Huckleberry as a tribute to Central Oregon ranching culture and hospitality.
Events

An exceptional private-events program

Under the leadership of Events Manager Alexa Marin, Hawkeye & Huckleberry Lounge has rapidly become one of Bend's premier destinations for private dining, corporate events, fundraisers, chef collaborations, ticketed experiences, nonprofit galas, and full-venue buyouts — a year-round destination for both local and national organizations.

  • Full restaurant buyouts, the private Huckleberry Lounge, semi-private canvas tents, and dedicated event spaces with stage and patio access — with capacity for large-scale corporate and social events.
  • Successfully executed dozens of private and ticketed events, from intimate VIP dinners to 200+ guest off-site experiences.
  • Developed recurring signature experiences including chef-collaboration dinners, Girl Dinner events, live-entertainment programming, nonprofit fundraisers, and large-scale corporate functions.
  • Built strong partnerships with regional businesses, influencers, nonprofits, and national brands.
