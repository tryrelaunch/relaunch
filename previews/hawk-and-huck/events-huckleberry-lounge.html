The Huckleberry Lounge

The Huckleberry Lounge is the largest private room at Hawk & Huck — a fully enclosed cocktail-and-dinner space with a dedicated bar, its own bartender, and just enough floor to dance when the night calls for it.

Built for corporate events, company holiday parties, receptions, fundraisers, and milestone celebrations. Accommodates up to 100 guests, with AV capabilities for presentations and meetings.
