The Bar Program

Cocktails built on the same Oregon the food is.

Our cocktail program leans on the same regional thinking as the kitchen — foraged huckleberries from the Cascades, honey from a beekeeper outside La Pine, herbs from our own garden. The Old Fashioned is built on Oregon bourbon. The Sour uses Oregon rye.

The patio is the most photographed seat in the lounge for a reason. Cascade view, heated and covered year-round, dog-friendly. Live music on Friday nights starting at 8 PM. Happy hour is the soft start to a long Bend evening.

I’m Your Huckleberry
Asked & answered

Happy hour questions.

What time is happy hour?

Every day from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM, in the bar and on the patio.

Where is happy hour served?

Bar seating and the outdoor patio only. Happy hour pricing is not available in the main dining room or the canvas tent.

Do you take reservations for happy hour?

Happy hour seating is first-come, first-served. For dining reservations after 5 PM, book through OpenTable.

Is there a separate happy hour menu?

Yes — a dedicated happy hour menu of wood-fired snacks, lounge plates, and signature cocktails at reduced prices, available only during happy hour hours.

Are there vegetarian options?

Yes — the Caesar Salad is on the happy hour menu, and the full dinner menu (with vegetarian plates like Beets & Burrata, Farm Greens, and Mushroom & Polenta) is also available in the bar and lounge.

Is the patio open year-round?

Yes — heated and covered seating is available during cooler months. Dog-friendly too.

Is happy hour family-friendly?

Yes — children are welcome in the lounge and on the patio. A kids menu is available.

Where is Hawkeye & Huckleberry located?

225 SW Century Drive, Bend, Oregon 97702. Free parking on-site.
