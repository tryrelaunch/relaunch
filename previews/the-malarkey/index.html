03
A homecoming

Brian Malarkey is coming home.

Brian
Malarkey
Chef & Owner

Brian Malarkey grew up on a working ranch in Central Oregon. He spent the next three decades building restaurants in Southern California and cooking on television. Now he is back.

The Malarkey is the restaurant he came home to build. A Pacific Northwest steakhouse with beef coming straight from the family ranch, fish off the Oregon coast, and the rest of the kitchen shaped alongside Chef Lalo, whose Latin sensibility runs through every plate.

Doors open at 555 NW 12th Avenue in 2026.
