Walter E. Nelson team — you've been the largest family-owned janitorial, chemical & paper distributor in the Pacific Northwest since 1945. Eighty years. Thirteen locations. And somebody Googling “janitorial supplies Portland” or “janitorial supplies Seattle” still doesn't find you on the first page.

A quick honest look at where you stand and what we changed.

What's not working on the current site
×
You rank #5 for “janitorial supplies Portland” — your HQ city. Four competitors with smaller footprints rank above you.
×
You rank #8 for “janitorial supplies Seattle” — the biggest market in the region. Page 1 still.
×
65% of all 270 monthly visits come from people searching your own name. Almost no demand for “janitorial supplies,” “cleaning supply distributor,” or category terms ever reaches the site.
×
Subdomain sprawl is splitting your SEO authority. ferndale.walterenelson.com, spokane.walterenelson.com, portland.walterenelson.com, shop.walterenelson.com — Google treats every one of those as a separate site. Your DR 20 is divided four ways before it can rank anything.
×
No structured data telling Google you're a 13-location distributor. No Organization schema, no LocalBusiness for the branches, no Service catalog. The hero is a slider with empty headlines (just spacers and product photos linking to PDFs).
What this rebuild fixes
✓
One unified site, one URL structure. Every branch lives under walterenelson.com/locations/[city]/ — SEO authority compounds instead of fragmenting across four subdomains.
✓
Full Organization + LocalBusiness schema for every branch. Google understands you have 13 distribution centers, not one fuzzy entity. Each city's “near me” search starts including you.
✓
Indexable category pages — Janitorial, Equipment, Packaging, Laundry, Service & Repair. Each one ranks for its own keyword instead of being buried under a hero slider.
✓
FAQ block written for AI search. When facility managers ask ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews “best janitorial supplier in Spokane,” WEN starts showing up in the answer.
✓
Edit anything by just asking. Type “update Salem branch hours” or “change the Portland main number” into the chat widget. Live in seconds. No webmaster.
