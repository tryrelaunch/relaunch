✗

Your Contact page has no phone, no address, no email. Just a form. Your phone number (931) 520-2427 only exists on the /location/ page — one page out of five. A customer who lands on your homepage cannot find a way to call you.

✗

Your phone number is not a tap-to-call link anywhere on the site. On a phone, that means nobody calling your restaurant just got handed a button to call you. They have to read the digits, switch apps, type them in.

✗

Zero structured data on the page. Google can't tell this is a restaurant. No Restaurant schema, no Menu schema, no opening hours, no events for the live music, no review markup. Other Cookeville restaurants show up in Google with menu items, star ratings, and event carousels. You show up as a generic blue link.

✗

You're CH Steak Lounge on the website — and “Char” on Facebook, Twitter, and Restaurant Guru. Two brands fighting each other for the same searches. Google Reviews fragmented across two business identities. Customers who hear “Char” from a friend can't find your website. Customers who know “CH Steak Lounge” land on a Facebook page branded differently.

✗

Six H1 tags on your homepage. No meta description. No Open Graph image. Six H1s means Google can't tell what your page is about. No meta description means Google writes its own SERP snippet. No Open Graph means every text message, every Facebook share, every iMessage of your URL looks broken — no image, no description.

✗

“upcoming events” and “chef specials” are H1 headers on your homepage with no content under them. Empty sections. Meanwhile your live-music calendar (“most nights after 9pm”) is invisible to Google — no Event schema, no calendar page, no way for somebody searching “live music cookeville this weekend” to ever find you.

Frequently asked

Questions guests ask us

Can I make a reservation at CH Steak Lounge?

Yes - request a reservation through the Reservations page, or call (931) 520-2427. We confirm by phone for parties of 6 or more.

How far in advance should I reserve?

Friday and Saturday nights and any night with a popular live-music act fill up fast - we recommend reserving at least a few days ahead. Weeknights you can usually book the same day.

Can I reserve a table for a special occasion?

Yes. Tell us in the reservation request - birthday, anniversary, work celebration, first date - and we'll make a note so the team takes care of you.

Do you host private events?

Yes. We host birthday dinners, rehearsal dinners, work parties, and other private events. Tell us the date, party size, and what you're celebrating - we'll put a package together.

Do you do carry-out?

Yes - carry-out is available during all open hours. Call (931) 520-2427 to place an order.
