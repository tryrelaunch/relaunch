What to expect

A pizza joint disguised as a neighborhood bar.

Desi's has been a Point Loma fixture for years. The bar's still the bar — beers, regulars, a TV in the corner — but the back has been Angry Pete's since 2022. The kitchen is small and the menu's tighter than Kearny Mesa: a handful of Detroit pans, the LooseBurger, a couple of rotating specials.

It's walk-in only. No reservations. You order at the counter, grab a seat at the bar or one of the high-tops, and the pizza comes out 20 minutes later. Crispy edges. Wisconsin brick to the corner. Three stripes of sauce on top, the way Detroit does it.

Cash, card, Apple Pay. Pickup orders go through Toast online — your pizza will be hot and bagged when you walk in.

Point Loma — questions

The honest answers.

Is this really inside a bar?
Yes. Desi's Bar is the front, Angry Pete's is the back. The bar is over 21. The pizza counter is open to anyone, but if you're under 21 you'll need to be seated at one of the food-only tables — let the host know when you walk in.
Why only nights?
The kitchen is small and shared with Desi's hours. Point Loma is a 5-night-a-week dinner operation. Kearny Mesa is the daytime spot — open 11am to 8pm, seven days a week, with the full menu.
Where do I park?
Street parking on Lytton and the cross streets. There's a small lot behind the building too. Weekends fill up — give yourself an extra few minutes if it's a Friday or Saturday night.
Can I order delivery?
Pickup only at Point Loma. The Detroit-style needs to be eaten hot — the caramelized cheese loses its texture inside a delivery box. Kearny Mesa is on DoorDash, but we honestly recommend coming in for it.
