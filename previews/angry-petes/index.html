The Story

A pop-up. A pandemic. A pizza obsession.

Pete Harbison grew up outside Detroit. He's a classically trained chef. He spent years in fine-dining kitchens. And then, like a lot of cooks during 2020, he started thinking about the food he actually wanted to eat — the Detroit pizza he couldn't find anywhere in San Diego.

He started slinging pies out of a pop-up — farmers markets, brewery pairings, festivals. Word got out fast. By 2022 there was a brick-and-mortar at Santee Lakes. Then Point Loma inside Desi's. Then a full sit-down spot in Kearny Mesa, in a converted Taco Bell of all things, with arcade games and ten taps of San Diego craft beer.

Detroit-style isn't a gimmick here. The dough proves for 48 hours. The pans are seasoned steel from Detroit. The cheese is Wisconsin brick, layered to the edge so it caramelizes against the pan into a crispy frico crust. It's the same pizza Pete grew up on, made the way it's supposed to be made.

Questions

The usual ones, answered.

What is Detroit-style pizza?
Rectangular, deep-dish, baked in a seasoned steel pan. The crust is light and airy from high-hydration dough. Cheese goes all the way to the edge so it caramelizes against the metal into a crispy frico crust. Sauce is ladled on top of the cheese in stripes — not under. We bake ours in 8×10 pans with 80% hydration dough and Wisconsin brick.
Where are the locations?
Point Loma at 2734 Lytton St — inside Desi's Bar, walk to the back. Phone (619) 224-6409. Kearny Mesa at 5335 Overland Ave — full restaurant, ten taps, arcade. Phone (619) 415-3477. Yes, the Santee Lakes spot closed in November 2024.
Do you take reservations?
First-come first-served at both locations. For large groups (8+) at Kearny Mesa, call ahead and we'll do our best. For pickup, order through Toast and we'll have it ready when you walk in.
Do you cater?
Yes — office lunches, parties, events. Minimum 4 pies. Call Kearny Mesa at (619) 415-3477 with 24+ hours notice and we'll build a custom order. We've fed everything from 12-person sales kickoffs to 200-guest weddings.
Are the pizzas big enough to share?
A single 8×10 pan is 8 generous square slices. Plenty for two hungry adults, or four people who are also ordering sides. Most groups order one pan per two people.
Are you gluten-free or vegetarian friendly?
We've got vegetarian options on every menu — the Spartan can be made meat-free, the Detroit Classic is veg by default, and we run weekly vegetable specials. We don't currently offer a gluten-free crust because the Detroit-style is defined by the high-hydration dough and we won't compromise on it.
