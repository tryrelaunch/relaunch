The Menu

Detroit, by the pan.

Signature pans · 8×10 Detroit-style

The Pizzas

Every pan is 8×10 inches, hand-pressed, 80% hydration. Eight square slices per pan. Wisconsin brick cheese all the way to the edge — that's where the crispy caramelized crust comes from. Most folks order one pan per two people.

Ezzo SS Pepperoni
$22
Full pan with Ezzo old-world cup-and-char pepperoni from Columbus, OH. Curls into crispy little oil-cups. The most-ordered pizza on the menu and it's not close.
The Spartan
$23
Italian sausage, caramelized onion, roasted green pepper, fresh mozzarella, Wisconsin brick. Pete's homage to the corner spot he grew up on in Michigan.
Habanero BBQ Chicken
$24
House BBQ sauce with a habanero kick, slow-cooked chicken, red onion, cilantro, mozzarella. The Detroit pan turns the BBQ into an almost-burnt caramel on the edges.
Coney Dog Pizza
$25
Sliced Coney dogs, Detroit-style chili, three stripes of mustard, raw white onion. Sells out every Friday. Yes it's a real Detroit thing.
Detroit Classic Red Top
$20
No frills, all crunch. Brick cheese to the corner, three stripes of San Marzano on top. The way Buddy's makes it in Detroit. Order it once and you'll get it.
Margherita Detroit
$21
Fresh mozzarella, hand-torn basil, San Marzano, finishing olive oil. The vegetarian crowd-pleaser. Light, balanced, somehow even better cold the next day.
Detroit-style sandwich menu

Burgers & Dogs

The non-pizza side of the menu. Detroit-style means steamed buns, loose meat, all-beef dogs, and the kind of toppings the Midwest gets right. Add fries for $4.

Detroit LooseBurger
$5
Loose-meat smash burger on a steamed bun. Pickles, mustard, raw onion. Five bucks. Goes with everything.
Coney Dog
$7
All-beef dog, Detroit chili, mustard, raw white onion, soft steamed bun. The way it's served at the original Coney Island in Detroit since 1917.
Plain Hot Dog
$6
All-beef dog, soft bun, your choice of fixings. Ketchup, mustard, relish, sport peppers, kraut, raw onion — build it your way.
Add a side of fries

Twice-fried, sea salt, served hot. To any sandwich or hot dog order.
