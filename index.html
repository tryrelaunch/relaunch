Common website problems
we fix for free

We hear these from local business owners every week. If any of this sounds familiar, we can help.

It takes too long to load

Customers on their phones won't wait more than 3 seconds. A slow site means lost calls, lost bookings, and lost revenue — every single day.

It doesn't look right on phones

If your site was built more than 5 years ago, there's a good chance it looks broken on mobile. That's where most of your customers are searching.

Google isn't ranking you

Missing meta descriptions, no schema, no sitemap. Your competitors show up first not because their business is better — but because their site is set up better.

Your reviews aren't on your site

You've worked hard for those Google reviews. They should be front and center — not something a customer has to go searching for.

Making a simple update takes forever

Updating hours or adding a photo shouldn't require logging into three systems or waiting a week for your web person to respond.

It doesn't represent your business well

You've built something you're proud of. Your website should reflect that — not look like it was made in 2010 by whoever was cheapest at the time.
